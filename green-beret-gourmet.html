Green Beret Gourmet

This highly sought after, impossible to obtain book is probably the most hardcore cook book ever published. I spent several years locating a copy of this.

NOTE: Some pages in the copy of this book have been left out, such as blank pages for making your own notes on. This will affect the actual index listing at the front of the book and some of the page references will be off by a page or two. Several more detailed index listings and other non-essential pages at the rear of the original book have also been left out to help reduce the overall size of the PDF file. But rest assured - every recipe and every drawing in the original is here!

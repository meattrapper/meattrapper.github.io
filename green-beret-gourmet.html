Green Beret Gourmet

This highly sought after, impossible to obtain book is probably the most hardcore cook book ever published. After a long absence, it will be returning soon! Stay tuned for updates!

This book was written by a Special Forces soldier who served in Vietnam. It's all here - the nitty gritty of how to prepare almost any type of wild game, critter or insects. Tips on dynamite fishing, cooking rats, snakes - and some you'll just have to see for yourself.

The no holds barrred content is matched only by the one of a kind artwork that is sprinkled throughout the book. Trust me - this book is a highly sought after classic. It's one that will not only entertain, it might just save your butt and fill your belly when things have gone belly up!
